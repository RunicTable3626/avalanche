Avalanche is in early alpha and far from ready for prime time. Our alpha testing server is invitation only for now; if you want an invite for testing, reach out to lincolnq on our Github.

That said, we won't stop you from starting your own server and playing around with it. Dev instructions are in the Github. We're also working through building out organizer getting-started docs, linked below.
