---
title: "Avalanche"
---

We're standing on the shoulders of [Signal](https://signal.org) to build the
best organizing platform for in-person coordination: conferences, canvassing, local organizing, and anything else.

Avalanche starts from Signal-quality encrypted messaging as the base, and
atop that enables rapidly-built, well-integrated organizing tools: group auto- and self-join, browse attendees, team assignments and subgroups, volunteer management, 
Q&A bots, and whatever else you can dream (and AI can implement!), all integrated into one mobile app that is as easy to use as Signal.

Avalanche is in early alpha, and not quite ready for prime time yet.
