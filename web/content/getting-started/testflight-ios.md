Avalanche isn't on the App Store yet, so on iPhone and iPad you install the beta through Apple's TestFlight.

<p><a class="btn btn-primary" href="https://testflight.apple.com/join/Cbz4KHc7" rel="noopener">Join the TestFlight beta</a></p>

## Install it

1. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store if you don't already have it.
2. Tap the **Join the TestFlight beta** link above **on your iPhone or iPad**.
3. In TestFlight, tap **Accept**, then **Install**.
4. Open Avalanche from your home screen.

Beta builds expire after 90 days; update through TestFlight when prompted.
